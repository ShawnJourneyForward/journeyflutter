# Journey Forward — Screen-by-Screen Screenshot Guide

A complete walkthrough of every screen in the app, in capture order, so you can
screenshot each one and assemble a PDF showing what it does. Each **▢ Shot**
is one screenshot to take. Tap-targets that open another screen are marked → .

**App structure at a glance:** a 6-tab bottom bar — **Home · Progress · Toolkit ·
Journal · Planner · Profile** — with ~30 deeper screens opened from those tabs.

> ⚠️ **The Journal tab is screenshot-protected** (FLAG_SECURE) — the OS will block
> screenshots there and on its sub-screens. To capture those for the PDF you'll
> need a second device photographing the screen, or temporarily disabling the
> protection in a debug build. Every other screen captures normally.

---

## 0. First-run / entry screens

These only appear in specific states — capture them first on a fresh install if
you want them in the PDF.

**▢ Shot 1 — Onboarding** (`/onboarding`, first launch only)
First-run setup. Welcome pages with trust pills ("No account needed", "Zero
tracking"), then steps to set: username, sober/quit date, daily spend + currency,
and notification preferences. *(Multi-page — capture each step.)*

**▢ Shot 2 — Lock screen** (`/lock`, only if a PIN/biometric lock is set)
PIN pad / fingerprint prompt shown when the app opens locked.

**▢ Shot 3 — Welcome dialog** ("A note from Shawn") — one-time popup on first Home visit.

**▢ Shot 4 — Safety disclaimer dialog** — one-time medical/crisis disclaimer with a "Get crisis help" button.

## 1. HOME tab  (`/home`)

Scroll top to bottom and capture:

Minutes / Seconds** counter and a daily serif line.

**▢ Shot 7 — Journey card** — milestone path (Day 0 → 7 → 30 → 90 → 180 → 365). **→ Milestone screen.**

**▢ Shot 9 — Intention card** — set today's intention/check-in.

**▢ Shot 10 — Gratitude card** — add a gratitude note for today.

**▢ Shot 11 — My Reason card** — your personal reason for staying sober.

**▢ Shot 13 — Weekly Goals card** *(only if goals are set)* — checklist of weekly goals.

**▢ Shot 14 — Daily Missions card** — 3 small daily missions to tick off.

**▢ Shot 15 — Check-in card** — four quick-log buttons, each opens a bottom sheet:
- **▢ Shot 16 — Log a Craving** sheet (intensity, trigger, notes)

**▢ Shot 21 — Recovery banner** — body-healing progress. **→ Recovery timeline.**

## Capture checklist (summary)

- **6 bottom-tab screens:** Home, Progress, Toolkit, Journal, Planner, Profile
- **~30 deeper screens** + several bottom sheets and dialogs
- **~71 numbered shots** total for a complete PDF (skip the conditional ones that
  don't apply to your data, e.g. Money card if no spend set)
- **Shareable cards** worth featuring: Milestone, Weekly Care Summary, Planner
  Share, 100-Day Challenge
- **Blocked by FLAG_SECURE:** everything under the Journal tab (Shots 43–50) —
  photograph the screen with another device if you need them

### Tip for a clean PDF
Capture on one device at one resolution, in light mode, with the same demo
profile (a nice round sober-day count and a set daily spend so the money/streak
cards look full). Group the PDF by the 6 tabs above — it mirrors how a reviewer
or store visitor actually navigates the app.
