Support

Help, without the maze.

For questions, bug reports, privacy concerns, and Android launch updates.

Contact Shawn

Email shawn@journeyforward.app. If you are reporting a bug, include your device model, Android version, app version, what you expected, and what happened.

Android launch

Journey Forward for Android is coming soon. It is being prepared carefully, with privacy and trust reviewed before release.

Backups and exports

Backups and exports are user-initiated. If you create one, keep the file somewhere private and secure.

Delete app data

Use the app's reset or delete options where available, or uninstall the app from your device. Also review your device backup settings if you use operating system backups.

Urgent help

If you may be in immediate danger or experiencing a medical emergency, contact local emergency services now. Journey Forward is not an emergency service.
