FAQ

How do I delete my app data?

Use the app’s reset / delete options in settings where available, or uninstall the app from your device. If device backups are enabled by the operating system, backup copies may be governed by your device settings.

How do backups and exports work?

Backups and exports are user-initiated. If you create one, the app generates a file for you to save or share. Once exported, that file is controlled by where you place it.

Does Journey Forward upload my journal?

No automatic journal upload exists in the app. Journey Forward has no account system, analytics, advertising, telemetry, or built-in cloud sync.

Is Journey Forward medical treatment?

No. Journey Forward is a self-support and reflection tool. It does not replace professional medical advice, therapy, crisis support, or emergency services.

If you need urgent help

If you may be in immediate danger or experiencing a medical emergency, contact local emergency services now. Journey Forward can support reflection, but it is not an emergency service.
