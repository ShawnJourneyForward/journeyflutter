Privacy

Your privacy is part of the product.

Journey Forward is designed as a local-first recovery companion. It does not require accounts, collect analytics, run ads, or sell personal data.

Overview

Journey Forward is built for reflection, grounding, and gentle progress. The app is designed to work offline and does not include account registration, analytics, advertising, telemetry, remote configuration, or built-in cloud sync.

What stays local

The app may store your sobriety start date, profile settings, check-ins, journal entries, gratitude notes, pledges, reasons, milestones, slips, cravings, thoughts, activities, sleep logs, and user-created history on your device.

What we do not collect

  • No accounts
  • No analytics
  • No ads
  • No telemetry
  • No sale of personal data
  • No automatic journal upload

Exports and sharing

If you choose to create a backup, export, share card, or support email, that is your action. Once exported or emailed, that file or message is governed by where you send or store it.

Device backups

Journey Forward does not operate its own cloud sync. Device-level backup behavior may depend on your operating system and app build configuration, so privacy-sensitive users should also review their Android backup settings.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact shawn@journeyforward.app.

Important

Journey Forward is not medical treatment.

Journey Forward is a self-support and reflection tool. It is not a replacement for professional medical advice, therapy, crisis care, or emergency services. If you may be in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or a qualified crisis resource now.
