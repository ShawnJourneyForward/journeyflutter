Privacy

Your privacy is part of the product.

Journey Forward is designed as a local-first recovery companion. It does not require accounts, collect analytics, run advertising, or sell personal data.

Overview

Journey Forward is built for reflection, grounding, and progress tracking. The app is intentionally offline-first and does not include cloud sync, user accounts, analytics, advertising, telemetry, or hidden tracking.

What the app stores locally

The app stores recovery-related information on the device so features can work, including sobriety start date, check-ins, journal content, gratitude, pledges, milestones, reasons, settings, and user-created history.

What we do not collect

  • No account registration
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising profiles
  • No sale of personal data
  • No cloud sync of recovery entries

Local storage and device backups

Recovery data is stored locally inside the app. Depending on the device and Android backup settings chosen by the user, the operating system may include app data in device backups. Journey Forward does not operate its own cloud sync service.

Manual exports and sharing

If a person chooses to create a backup, export, or share card, that action creates a user-controlled file or share event. Those files are outside the app once exported and are governed by where the user sends or stores them.

Current technical note

Current app builds may request internet access for runtime font delivery until fonts are fully bundled locally. This does not upload recovery entries, create accounts, or enable analytics.

Support emails and website contact

If you email support, the contents of that email are handled by your email provider and Journey Forward support. You can contact support at support@journeyforward.app or privacy at privacy@journeyforward.app.

Children’s privacy

Journey Forward is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not require accounts or analytics, it does not build user profiles.

Emergency and medical disclaimer

Journey Forward is a self-support and reflection tool. It is not a replacement for professional medical advice, therapy, crisis support, or emergency care. If you may be in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or a qualified crisis resource now.
