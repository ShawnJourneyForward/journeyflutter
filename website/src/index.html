Inside the toolkit

Practical, named, ready when you need them.

Real features built for real recovery days — not generic wellness fluff.

Live time counter

Days, hours, minutes, seconds — every moment is a win you can watch tick by, right on the home screen.

Pre-craving plan

Write the moves before the storm. When a craving hits, your own plan is one tap away — calm, prepared, ready.

Future letters

Write to your future self. Sealed until the date you choose — a message of strength waiting for the day you'll need it.

Cravings heatmap

Notice the patterns — which day, which hour. Quiet, judgement-free awareness so you can be ready next time.

Encrypted backups

Passphrase-protected .jfwbk files. Your data, on your terms — local-first, portable, never on someone else's server.

Biometric or PIN lock

Fingerprint, face, or a 4-digit PIN. Your reflections stay private, even if your phone is in someone else's hand.

Plant that grows with you

A quiet botanical companion. 100 hand-drawn stages of growth across your journey — not a scoreboard, a witness.

Breathing & grounding

Box breathing, 4-7-8, and slow-down patterns. Visual ring, gentle haptics, no countdown noise — just breath.

Thought records

CBT-inspired worksheets for the thoughts that pull you backwards. Spot the distortion, find the kinder reframe.
