Share cards

Milestones can be shared. Your journal never is.

Journey Forward includes milestone share cards when someone wants to mark progress outwardly. The important distinction is deliberate: sharing is user-controlled, while private reflections stay private.

  • Milestone cards are intentionally created by the user.
  • Journal entries are never uploaded automatically.
  • Optional community sharing, if it exists later, belongs outside the private core of the app.

Private by design

Your recovery is not a data product.

Journey Forward is built without accounts, analytics, advertising, telemetry, or cloud sync. Recovery entries live locally on the device, with user-created exports only when someone chooses to make them.
