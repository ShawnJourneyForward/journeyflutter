milestoneOneDay	One Day
milestoneOneDayShort	1 Day
milestoneOneDayBenefit	One full day. Alcohol typically clears from the body within this window. For many people, tonight's sleep — though sometimes restless — feels different from the nights that came before.
milestoneThreeDays	Three Days
milestoneThreeDaysShort	3 Days
milestoneThreeDaysBenefit	Most alcohol metabolites have left your body. The brain's GABA system is recalibrating — this can bring restlessness, but it means your nervous system is finding its natural balance again. Hydration is improving.
milestoneOneWeek	One Week
milestoneOneWeekShort	1 Week
milestoneOneWeekBenefit	One full week. Many people start to notice sharper thinking, more natural energy, and better hydration around this stage. Your body has had a meaningful stretch of recovery time.
milestoneTwoWeeks	Two Weeks
milestoneTwoWeeksShort	2 Weeks
milestoneTwoWeeksBenefit	Two weeks. For many people, anxiety begins to stabilise and sleep deepens. The early-recovery storm often starts to soften here, though every person's timeline is different.
milestoneOneMonth	One Month
milestoneOneMonthShort	1 Month
milestoneOneMonthBenefit	One month. Many people describe meaningful gains in clarity and emotional steadiness at this point. Cravings can become easier to observe without acting on them.
milestoneTwoMonths	Two Months
milestoneTwoMonthsShort	2 Months
milestoneTwoMonthsBenefit	Two months. Research suggests the prefrontal cortex — responsible for decisions, impulse control, and empathy — begins to recover meaningfully around this stage. Many people see improvements in cholesterol levels. You are physically and neurologically different from who you were.
milestoneThreeMonths	Three Months
milestoneThreeMonthsShort	3 Months
milestoneThreeMonthsBenefit	Three months. Skin can look clearer, sleep can feel deeper, and concentration often continues to sharpen. You have built real momentum.
milestoneSixMonths	Six Months
milestoneSixMonthsShort	6 Months
milestoneSixMonthsBenefit	Six months. Many people report that around this point, sobriety has begun to feel like part of who they are — not just a goal they're chasing.
milestoneOneYear	One Year
milestoneOneYearShort	1 Year
milestoneOneYearBenefit	One year. This is a profound milestone. Many people describe genuine, lasting changes in how they feel and how they relate to themselves. The cumulative gains of a year without alcohol are real — and they are yours.
