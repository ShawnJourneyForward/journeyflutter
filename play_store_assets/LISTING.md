## Full description (4000 chars max)

```
Your recovery is yours. Journey Forward is the private one — a sobriety
companion that never asks for an account, never shows an ad, never sells
your data, and never puts your streak behind a paywall. Everything works
offline, everything stays on your phone, and everything is free. Forever.

WHATEVER YOU'RE STEPPING AWAY FROM
Alcohol, smoking, vaping, cannabis, gambling, pornography — or your own
journey. Pick yours and the app speaks to it, including a healing timeline
of what your body and mind are doing right now: day 3, day 14, day 90.

WHEN AN URGE HITS
• Ride the Wave — a 10-minute urge timer with a breathing visual. Urges
  feel permanent; they aren't. Outlast one and it's counted as a win.
• Your pre-craving plan — three steps you wrote for yourself, shown first.
• "Last time at this intensity, walking helped" — your own history,
  surfaced when your willpower is busiest.
• One-tap crisis lines for South Africa, US, UK, EU, AU, NZ and Canada.
• An SOS widget on your home screen that jumps straight to the urge timer,
  even past your app lock.

WATCH YOURSELF HEAL
• Live sober clock — days, hours, minutes, seconds.
• Money saved, with your own savings goal.
• Dense early milestones (day 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 10, 14, 21…) because the
  first weeks are the hardest and deserve the most celebration.
• Your tender hours — the app learns when your cravings cluster and
  helps you plan for that window. On your phone. Never in a cloud.
• Beautiful milestone cards to share — or keep.

BUILT FOR THE HARD DAYS, TOO
A slip doesn't delete your story. Journey Forward keeps every day you
fought as part of your lifetime record, helps you understand what
happened, and starts the next streak with you.

DAILY GROUND
Morning pledge, gratitude, evening check-in, mood journal with voice
input, CBT thought tools, breathing exercises, calming ambient sounds,
letters to your future self, and a vision board for the life you're
building.

PRIVATE BY ARCHITECTURE
• No account. No sign-up. No email.
• Data encrypted on your device (Android Keystore).
• Optional PIN or fingerprint lock.
• Encrypted backups you control — export to your own storage.
• No analytics, no trackers, no ads. We can't see your data. Nobody can.

Journey Forward is built by one person in recovery's corner, and it's
free forever. Every feature is already yours.

One day at a time. Onward.

Journey Forward is a self-help companion, not a medical device. If you
may be physically dependent on alcohol or another substance, please
speak to a doctor before stopping — withdrawal can be dangerous. In a
crisis, contact your local emergency number.
```
